Introduction

This privacy policy describes how information is handled when you access or interact with this website. The policy outlines data categories that may be collected, purposes for processing, retention periods, individual rights, and security measures implemented to protect information.

Information Categories Collected

When you use the contact form on this website, the following categories of personal information may be collected:

  • Identifiers: name and email address provided in the contact form
  • Communication content: message text submitted through the contact form
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, and access timestamps collected automatically through server logs
  • Website interaction data: pages viewed, time spent on pages, and navigation patterns

Sources of Information

Information is collected directly from individuals who voluntarily submit contact forms. Technical data is gathered automatically through standard web server operations when you access website pages. No information is obtained from third-party data brokers or external sources.

Purposes of Data Processing

Information collected serves the following purposes:

  • Responding to inquiries submitted through the contact form
  • Maintaining records of correspondence for reference
  • Operating and maintaining website functionality
  • Analyzing website usage patterns to improve content organization and navigation
  • Detecting and addressing technical issues affecting website performance
  • Complying with applicable legal obligations

Information is not used for targeted advertising, behavioral profiling, or automated decision-making that produces legal or significant effects.

Data Retention Periods

Contact form submissions are retained for two years from the date of submission unless deletion is requested earlier. Server logs containing technical data are maintained for ninety days. Information may be retained longer if required by legal obligations or legitimate operational needs.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Personal information is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties for their marketing purposes. Information may be disclosed in the following limited circumstances:

  • To service providers who perform website hosting or technical maintenance functions under contractual confidentiality obligations
  • When required by law, legal process, or governmental request
  • To protect rights, property, or safety when necessary

No personal information is transferred to third parties for targeted advertising purposes.

Individual Rights

Subject to applicable law, individuals may have rights regarding their personal information:

  • Access: Request information about what personal data is held
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of personal information subject to legal retention requirements
  • Restriction: Request limitation of processing under certain conditions
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Data portability: Receive personal information in a structured format where technically feasible

To exercise these rights, contact us using the information provided at the beginning of this policy. Identity verification may be required before fulfilling requests. Responses will be provided within timeframes required by applicable law.

Notice at Collection for California Residents

California residents have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA). This section provides required notice at collection regarding personal information categories:

  • Identifiers (name, email, IP address): collected for communication and website operation purposes; retained for two years
  • Internet activity information: collected for website functionality and improvement; retained for ninety days

California residents may exercise rights to know, delete, correct, and limit use of sensitive personal information. This website does not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising. Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals are recognized and honored.

Children's Information

This website does not knowingly collect personal information from children under thirteen years of age, in accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). The website is not directed to children. If it comes to our attention that information has been collected from a child under thirteen without verified parental consent, that information will be deleted promptly.

Security Measures

Technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of data transmission using secure protocols
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel
  • Regular security assessments of website infrastructure
  • Secure storage practices for collected information

While reasonable security measures are employed, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be revised periodically to reflect changes in information practices or legal requirements. The last updated date at the beginning of this policy indicates the most recent revision. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact for Privacy Inquiries

Questions regarding this privacy policy or information practices may be directed to the contact information provided at the beginning of this document. For more information, please use the contact details above.
