Introduction

This cookie policy explains how cookies and similar technologies are used on this website. The policy describes the types of cookies deployed, their purposes, duration, and how you can manage or decline cookie usage.

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. These files contain information that is read by the website on subsequent visits. Cookies serve various functions including maintaining session state, remembering preferences, and collecting usage information. Similar technologies such as web beacons and local storage may also be employed for comparable purposes.

Types of Cookies Used

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are required for basic website operation. They enable core functionality such as page navigation, access to secure areas, and form submission. The website cannot function properly without these cookies. Because they are essential for website operation, these cookies do not require consent under applicable regulations.

Examples include session identifiers that maintain your connection during a single visit and security cookies that prevent unauthorized access attempts.

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember choices you make to provide enhanced features and personalization. These may include language preferences, regional settings, or display preferences you have selected. These cookies improve your experience but are not essential for basic website operation.

Duration: Functional cookies typically remain on your device for the duration of your session or up to one year for persistent preferences.

Limited Analytics Cookies

This website uses limited analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with content. These cookies collect aggregated information about page views, navigation patterns, and time spent on pages. The data collected helps improve website organization and content structure.

Analytics cookies on this website are configured to:

  • Collect anonymized data only
  • Exclude personally identifiable information
  • Aggregate data across all visitors
  • Support website improvement purposes only

Duration: Analytics cookies may remain on your device for up to two years.

Cookies Not Used on This Website

This website does not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or social media cookies. No cookies are deployed for the following purposes:

  • Targeted or behavioral advertising
  • Cross-site tracking or user profiling
  • Third-party advertising networks
  • Social media platform integration or sharing
  • Remarketing or retargeting campaigns

Managing Cookie Preferences

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through browser settings. You can configure your browser to:

  • Block all cookies
  • Accept only first-party cookies
  • Receive notification before cookies are placed
  • Delete existing cookies from your device

Browser help sections provide instructions for managing cookies in specific browsers. Be aware that blocking strictly necessary cookies may affect website functionality.

Cookie Consent Management

Upon your first visit to this website, you may encounter a cookie notice providing options to accept or decline non-essential cookies. Your preferences are recorded and respected during subsequent visits. You can modify your consent choices at any time by accessing cookie settings available in the website footer or by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting the site.

Global Privacy Control

This website recognizes and honors Global Privacy Control (GPC) signals transmitted by your browser or extension. When a GPC signal is detected, non-essential cookies are not deployed, and your opt-out preference is respected automatically.

Third-Party Cookies

This website does not permit third-party cookies from external advertising networks, analytics services, or social media platforms. Any cookies placed originate from this website domain only. No external parties have access to place cookies through this website.

Cookie Retention and Deletion

Different cookie types have different lifespans:

  • Session cookies: Deleted automatically when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for specified periods ranging from days to years, depending on cookie type and purpose

You can delete all cookies from your device at any time using browser settings. Consult your browser documentation for specific deletion instructions.

Updates to Cookie Policy

This cookie policy may be revised periodically to reflect changes in cookie usage or legal requirements. The last updated date at the top of this policy indicates the most recent revision. Significant changes will be communicated through a notice on the website. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions About Cookies

If you have questions about how cookies are used on this website or need assistance managing cookie preferences, contact us using the information provided at the beginning of this policy. For more information, please use the contact details above.
